Mingyu (he/him) is interested in the environmental pathobiology of cardiometabolic diseases. His work focuses on understanding how prenatal and perinatal environmental exposures (e.g., ambient air pollutants, and metals) may shape disease risks and influence cardiometabolic health across the life span. 

Mingyu is participating in multiple NIH-funded studies, including the Boston Birth Cohort, Environmental influences on Child Health Outcomes (ECHO) project, Nurture cohort, Project Viva, etc. His work has been covered by mainstream media outlets including The Washington Post, The New York Times, and Reuters.
